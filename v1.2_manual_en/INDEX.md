# Black Mirror Board v1.2 Official Manual

> The complete guide for first-time Black Mirror Board users.
> Draw, speak (the terminal), cast spells — everything this tool does, from
> first draw to authoring and sharing your own magic, is covered here.

---

## What can this tool do?

- **Draw freely like a blackboard** — circles, squares, triangles, arrows, text, freehand
- **Cast "spells" from the terminal to transform the canvas** — single commands reshape the board
- **Author your own spells · share them with anyone** — spells travel as JSON payloads
- **Summon images by keyword** — `fetch-img sea 6` tiles six ocean photos into a grid
